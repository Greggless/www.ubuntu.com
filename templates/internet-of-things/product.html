Migrate from Homegrown with Canonical

Taking a one-shot release to a successful product is all about the platform.

When building your IoT device, why spend resource on creating a whole OS from scratch? Instead it should be about how you provide a great solution to your customers on top of a proven platform.

Homegrown solutions can help you launch your product but that is just a small part of the problem. Very soon the need arises for security updates, bug fixing, expandability and enhancements. Let Canonical take care of providing the most secure, robust, and reliable solution so that you can spend more time on your innovative ideas.

Fits your development strategy

A platform the works with your entire product lifecycle.

Building an IoT or Intelligent Edge device starts with your great idea. Bringing that idea to life requires a complex hardware and software integration which includes choosing your hardware, creating the OS, building the infrastructure, and deploying, but it doesn’t end there. Software updates, security and bug fixes, repairs and servicing, and migration are all parts of the product life-cycle after the device is in the market and this is where Canonical can help with a large list of supported hardware or talk to us about your own.

A standard and familiar platform

Ubuntu is the world’s most popular Linux desktop and public cloud OS and is the innovator’s choice of platform for IoT. The same, open source operating system runs everywhere giving you a familiar environment regardless of your target hardware.

Extensive hardware support

Choosing the right hardware for your IoT device is an important step, not only because you need the right functionality, you also need the most powerful OS to go with it. Ubuntu has been ported to a wide range of hardware devices and architectures from x86 to ARM making your choice much simpler.

Works with your existing process

The snapcraft development platforms fits with your existing CI/CD process meaning less interruptions and a fast start.

Add Ubuntu, snaps and your own software for a complete IoT solution

Snaps are the universal package format for the world of IoT and beyond. They are transforming the way we install and update applications by bundling their dependencies and configuration, running in a confined area, and simplifying the device update story.
